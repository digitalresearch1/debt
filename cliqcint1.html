Who Can Participate?

Please proceed only if the following is true for you:

  • You currently have unsecured debt (e.g., credit card debt, unsecured personal loans, or private student loans).
  • You have $15,000+ in eligible debt.
  • You have $30,000+ household income.
  • You are not currently enrolled in a debt relief / debt settlement / debt management program.
  • You are willing to complete a short questionnaire on your device.
  • You can upload a screenshot of the confirmation/thank-you page after submitting the secure form.

If you do not want to continue, you can click Abandon Survey at any time.

How To Participate

Important

When you click Continue inside the form, please wait a few seconds for the next questions to appear.

You will typically go through these steps:

  1. Select your debt amount using the slider (example: $15,000+), then click Continue.
  2. Enter your legal first and last name, then click Continue.
  3. Enter your date of birth and email, then click Continue.
  4. Enter your phone number (may require verification) and click Accept & Unlock Result.
  5. Reach the results page and click at least one offer to view details.
  6. There is no obligation to sign up or enroll. You are only viewing the offer details for feedback.
  7. Take a screenshot of the confirmation/thank-you page after submitting the secure form.

We verify your screenshot using OCR (text detection). If confirmation keywords are not detected, the next step stays locked.

Use Real Information

Please enter your real first name, last name, and current address so the website can verify your identity and display results. This verification is a soft check and does not affect your credit score.

No SSN is requested. However, the form may not submit or you may not see results if your identity/credit information cannot be verified.
