What is a Home Warranty?

Understanding how home warranties protect your biggest investment

Peace of Mind for Your Home

A home warranty is a service contract that covers the repair or replacement of major home systems and appliances that break down over time due to normal wear and tear.

Unlike homeowners insurance, which covers damage from external events like fires or storms, a home warranty protects you from the unexpected breakdowns that happen every day.

  • Covers breakdowns from normal wear and tear
  • One simple service fee covers the repair
  • Access to vetted, licensed contractors
  • Protects your budget from surprise expenses
  • Increases home value for resale

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about home warranties and Choice coverage

What's the difference between a home warranty and homeowners insurance?

Homeowners insurance covers damage from external events like fires, storms, and theft. A home warranty covers breakdowns from normal wear and tear of your home's systems and appliances.

How quickly can I use my coverage?

There's a standard 30-day waiting period, but coverage begins immediately for pre-existing conditions! Claims can be filed 24/7 online or by phone.

Do I get to choose my contractor?

Choice assigns one of their 25,000+ pre-screened, licensed contractors. However, you can request a specific provider, and they'll vet them for inclusion in their network.

What's not covered?

Pre-existing conditions, improper installation, code violations, and cosmetic damage. See the full policy on the Choice website for complete details and exclusions.

Can I cancel if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! Choice offers a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied within the first month, they'll refund your full purchase price.

Do you cover older homes?

Absolutely! Choice covers homes of any age. In fact, older homes often benefit most from a warranty due to aging systems and appliances.
