Frequently asked questions

Will checking eligibility affect my credit?

No hard credit pull is used to check eligibility. Discuss details with your specialist for your specific situation.

What debts are usually eligible?

Typically unsecured debts like credit cards, personal loans, or medical bills. Secured debts (auto, mortgage) usually don’t qualify.

How soon can I get help?

Initial review is fast. Program timelines vary. You’ll discuss specifics with a licensed partner before choosing.

Are there risks?

All programs have pros/cons and potential credit/collection impacts. Make sure you understand fees, timelines, and obligations before enrolling.
