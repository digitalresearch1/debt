App experience research study

Help us understand how new users experience this app for the first time

The purpose of this study is to understand how people who have not used TikTok before experience the app when they try it for the first time.

Because you are new to this type of app, your feedback helps us evaluate things like ease of sign-up, clarity of features, and overall first impressions.

There are no right or wrong answers — we are simply interested in your honest opinion based on your first experience.

What you’ll do

  • Click Click to download (the App Store will open)
  • Install the app
  • Open the app and complete the sign-up
  • Spend a short moment exploring the app
  • Return here to answer a few feedback questions
