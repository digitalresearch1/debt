Debt Offer Match Feedback Survey

Help us understand how clear and useful a debt-offer matching experience is.

Who Can Participate?

Please proceed if the following is true for you:

  • You currently have unsecured debt, preferably credit card or personal loan debt.
  • You are willing to complete a short debt-relief questionnaire on your device.
  • You can take and upload a screenshot of the results page showing your matched offers.

Note: Some participants may not qualify to continue based on their answers.

How To Participate

  1. Click "Open Website" below to open the debt matching website in a new tab.
  2. Move the debt amount slider to select your total debt amount (this study focuses on $15,000+).
  3. Select your debt type and your income bracket using your real situation.
  4. Complete the form and submit until you reach the offers/results page.
  5. On the results page, click at least one offer to view details (enrollment/calling is optional).
  6. Take a screenshot of the results/offers page.
  7. Return to this survey tab and upload your screenshot below for OCR verification, then answer a few short questions.

Important

The website you will use is a real third-party service that may match people with potential debt-help offers. We are not the website and we are not the debt companies shown.
