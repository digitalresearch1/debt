Help us understand how clear and useful a debt-offer matching experience is.

How To Participate

  1. Open the debt matching website using the button below (it opens in a new tab).
  2. The first screen you will see is a debt amount slider. Please move the slider to select the total amount you owe, then continue through the form. This study focuses on individuals with $15,000 or more in unsecured debt, so please answer accurately.
  3. When asked about debt type, select what applies to you (for example, credit card debt, which is the main focus of this study). When asked about income, select the bracket that matches your real situation.
  4. Complete the short form using real, accurate information (if the details are incorrect, the form may not submit or you may not reach the results/offers screen). Continue until you reach the results page (or are directed to an offer).
  5. On the results page, you may see one or more partner offers or you may be directed to an offer page. Please click at least one offer to view details and check whether it fits your needs. If you wish to enroll in or call any of the programs shown, you may do so to receive help with your situation.
  6. Take a screenshot of what you see after submitting the form (results page / offers / special offers).
  7. Upload your screenshot below. We use OCR to verify that you reached the offer/results screen.
  8. After verification, answer a few short questions about what you saw and your experience.

Important
